3-Step Workflow
From Idea to Digital Flipbook
✨
Step 1
Gemini
Generate story text + image description per page
→
🎨
Steps 2 + 3
Canva
Generate illustrations + layout pages with text
→
📖
Step 4
Heyzine
Export PDF → publish as shareable flipbook link
Step 01 · Gemini
Generate Your Story Text
1
Open gemini.google.com
Sign in with your Google account
2
Paste your story prompt
Be specific about grade, theme, and page format
3
Copy story + image descriptions
Gemini writes the text AND describes each page illustration
📝 Story Prompt Template
Write a storybook for Primary [grade] students about "[topic]", 8 pages, 2–3 sentences per page. For each page, add an image description in 【 】 for illustration.
💡 Add the moral or learning objective — Gemini will weave it naturally into the story
Step 02 · Gemini
Generate Illustrations
1
In Gemini, switch to image generation mode
Look for the image icon in the chat toolbar
2
Paste each page image description
Use the 【text】 from your story output, one page at a time
3
Download each illustration
Right-click → Save image → Name by page number (p1, p2…)
📝 Illustration Prompt Format
【Your page description here】, children’s storybook illustration style, soft pastel colours, child-friendly, clean composition, no text in image
💡 Keep the style phrase identical for every page — this ensures visual consistency across your whole book
Steps 03 & 04
Canva Layout → Heyzine Flipbook
1
Open a Canva book template
Search "storybook" or "children book" in Canva templates
2
Import illustrations + add story text
Replace placeholder images; paste text for each page
3
Export as PDF → Upload to heyzine.com
Free account — drag and drop your PDF to create flipbook
4
Copy the share link → Done!
Students open the flipbook on any device — no app needed
📖
heyzine.com
Free · No student account needed to view · Works on any device
🔗 Share the link with students — they can flip through the pages on their phones!

What Makes It Different?
NotebookLM vs ChatGPT
📓 NOTEBOOKLM
✅ Answers only from your uploaded files
✅ Cites exactly which source it used
✅ Never hallucinates outside your materials
✅ Perfect for lesson content, textbooks, syllabuses
"Your private AI that only reads what you give it"
vs
🌐 CHATGPT / GENERAL AI
⚠️ Trained on the entire internet
⚠️ May confidently give wrong answers
⚠️ Cannot reference your specific school materials
⚠️ Harder to verify what it's basing answers on
"Knows everything — including things that aren't true"
