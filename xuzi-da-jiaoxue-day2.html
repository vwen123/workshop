序自达教学

ProStart Teaching

自学Self-Study
提问Question

环 Ring学生做什么 · What Students Do老师做什么 · What Teachers Do
S 自学
Self-Study		带着问题，先自己挣扎
Struggle with the material independently first		设计讲义，巡场，不讲解
Design materials, observe — do not explain
Q 提问
Question		思考核心问题，写下答案
Think through the key question, write answers		设计好问题（最难的环）
Design the question — the hardest part
G 讨论
Discussion		小组碰撞，形成共识
Discuss, debate, build consensus		抽签指名，不救场
Draw lots, do not rescue
P 表达
Presentation		代表上台，说出小组想法
Present the group's thinking		评估理解，给有意义的回馈
Assess understanding, give meaningful feedback
S 统整
Synthesis		听老师归纳，补充盲点
Listen, fill in the gaps		重述、后设、延伸拓展
Restate, metacognition, extend learning

T H E   H E A R T   O F   T H E   L E S S O N

问题决定学生想多深

Your Questions Determine How Deep Students Think

O

客观 Objective

从课文里找到的 · From the text

文中说了什么？你看到什么？
What does the text say? What do you see?

R

反映 Reflective

自己的感受 / 联想 · Personal response

这让你想起什么？你有什么感觉？
What does this remind you of? How do you feel?

I

诠释 Interpretive

找意义、找原因 · Finding meaning

为什么会这样？这说明了什么？
Why is this so? What does this tell us?

D

决定 Decisional

行动 / 表态 / 创造 · Action & creation

如果是你，你会怎么做？
If it were you, what would you do?

C L O S I N G   ·   带走这个 · Take This with You

每个孩子的花期不同，
他们都值得被等待绽放的时刻。

Every child blooms in their own time.
Every one of them deserves to be waited for.

今天你带走的，不只是一份教案。
是一个相信——

学生可以自己想，
好的问题比好的答案更珍贵，
课堂可以是一个每个人都有位置的地方。

What you take home today is more than a lesson plan.
It is a belief — that students can think for themselves,
that a good question is worth more than a good answer,
that a classroom can be a place where everyone belongs.
